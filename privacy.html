Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2025

At HatchyByte, we value your trust. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your personal information when you interact with our website, mobile applications, and digital platforms. As a technology-driven company, your privacy and data protection are fundamental to how we build software and offer services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect certain personal information to provide better service and ensure platform security. This includes:

  • Contact Information such as your name, email address, and phone number when you register or reach out to us.
  • Usage Data such as pages visited, interactions made, IP addresses, and device type for analytics and optimization.
  • Additional Data such as survey responses, feedback submissions, or support requests that help us understand your needs.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used to deliver our services reliably and securely. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Manage user accounts and support features within our applications
  • Send you notifications, service announcements, or updates
  • Analyze how users engage with our products to improve performance and features
  • Respond to support tickets and offer customer service

3. Data Sharing

HatchyByte does not sell your personal data. However, we may share information with third-party vendors or tools (like hosting platforms, email services, or analytics tools) that help us deliver our services more efficiently. All partners are bound by confidentiality and data protection agreements.

4. Data Security

Security is a core part of our infrastructure. We implement encryption, firewalls, and access controls to prevent unauthorized access. Our systems are regularly updated and monitored to prevent data breaches or misuse.

5. Your Rights

You have full control over your data. At any time, you can request to:

  • Access the personal data we have stored
  • Request corrections or updates to your information
  • Withdraw consent or ask for complete deletion of your data

To exercise your rights, please email us at info@hatchybyte.com.

6. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience, remember user preferences, and gather insights on user behavior. You can disable or manage cookie preferences in your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect functionality on our website or app.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in law, technology, or business operations. When we do, we’ll post the updated policy with the latest revision date at the top of this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions, suggestions, or concerns about our privacy practices, we’re here to help. Please reach out to us at: info@hatchybyte.com
